<h1 style="text-align: center;">Mission Accomplished!</h1><p style="text-align: center;">MUR Motorsports is happy to announce that we successfully built a car, completed all 7 inspections and had our car run at dynamic events. The last 4 days at Calder Park have been intense; the team has had to dig deep, but we are happy to have accomplished our mission.</p><p style="text-align: center;">2024 has been a massive year, seeing our first car back on track at competition since 2019.</p><p style="text-align: center;">We could not have completed this without the support of the university, our sponsors, our friends and our family.&nbsp;Thank you to all who have supported us, and we can't wait for 2025.</p>
</section>

<div class="content-block" id="new_content_container_2903271_2903271">
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Our Mission</h2><p style="text-align: center;" class="text-center">Melbourne University Racing brings together a diverse cohort of students to design, build and race an electric race car every year&mdash; from scratch.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block bg-alt" id="component_5083346">
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Our Vision</h2><p style="text-align: center;">To excel in student motorsports while equipping our members with the skills, professionalism and industry readiness needed for successful careers.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block" id="component_5083349">
<p style="text-align: center;">The team consists of over 100 members, ranging in experience from first-year Undergraduates to final-year Master's students across all faculties in the university. We compete against other universities worldwide in Formula SAE and strive to do so while fostering a collaborative environment for all students.</p><p style="text-align: center;">Students involved in Melbourne University Racing gain invaluable skills that apply not only to their studies but to life beyond university. We hope to share our passions and skills beyond our team and create positive value for all students at the University of Melbourne</p><p style="text-align: center;">To learn more about us and our team, see the links below.</p>
</div>

<h2 style="text-align: center;">Our Vision</h2><p style="text-align: center;">To excel in student motorsports while equipping our members with the skills, professionalism and industry readiness needed for successful careers.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block" id="component_5083349">

We compete against other universities worldwide in Formula SAE and strive to do so while fostering a collaborative environment for all students.</p><p style="text-align: center;">Students involved in Melbourne University Racing gain invaluable skills that apply not only to their studies but to life beyond university. We hope to share our passions and skills beyond our team and create positive value for all students at the University of Melbourne</p><p style="text-align: center;">To learn more about us and our team, see the links below.</p>

<section class="content-block" id="component_5169966">
<h1 style="text-align: center;">Mission Accomplished!</h1><p style="text-align: center;">MUR Motorsports is happy to announce that we successfully built a car, completed all 7 inspections and had our car run at dynamic events. The last 4 days at Calder Park have been intense; the team has had to dig deep, but we are happy to have accomplished our mission.</p><p style="text-align: center;">2024 has been a massive year, seeing our first car back on track at competition since 2019.</p><p style="text-align: center;">We could not have completed this without the support of the university, our sponsors, our friends and our family.&nbsp;Thank you to all who have supported us, and we can't wait for 2025.</p>
</section>

<div class="content-block" id="new_content_container_2903271_2903271">
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Our Mission</h2><p style="text-align: center;" class="text-center">Melbourne University Racing brings together a diverse cohort of students to design, build and race an electric race car every year&mdash; from scratch.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block bg-alt" id="component_5083346">
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Our Vision</h2><p style="text-align: center;">To excel in student motorsports while equipping our members with the skills, professionalism and industry readiness needed for successful careers.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block" id="component_5083349">
<p style="text-align: center;">The team consists of over 100 members, ranging in experience from first-year Undergraduates to final-year Master's students across all faculties in the university. We compete against other universities worldwide in Formula SAE and strive to do so while fostering a collaborative environment for all students.</p><p style="text-align: center;">Students involved in Melbourne University Racing gain invaluable skills that apply not only to their studies but to life beyond university. We hope to share our passions and skills beyond our team and create positive value for all students at the University of Melbourne</p><p style="text-align: center;">To learn more about us and our team, see the links below.</p>
</div>
