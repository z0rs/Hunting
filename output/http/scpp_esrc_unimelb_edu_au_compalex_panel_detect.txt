<p>The Curriculum Policies Archive provides access to digital copies of Australian state curriculum policies collected as part of the ARC funded project <i>School Knowledge, Working Knowledge and the Knowing Subject: A review of state curriculum policies 1975-2005</i>. It contains over 450 policy documents developed by Australian state education departments and curriculum authorities from the early 1970s to the mid 2000s.</p>
